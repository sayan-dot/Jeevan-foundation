# Prana

**The Design Language of Jeevan**

Version: 0.1

---

## What is Prana?

Prana is the design language behind Jeevan.

It defines how every interface should look, feel, behave, and communicate.

Prana is more than a collection of colors, typography, components, and guidelines.

It is a philosophy for creating healthcare experiences that are calm, accessible, trustworthy, and human.

Every screen, animation, icon, illustration, and interaction within Jeevan is built using Prana.

---

## Why Prana?

Healthcare is one of the most personal experiences in a person's life.

People may open Jeevan when they are worried.

When they are sick.

When they are caring for a loved one.

When they simply need reassurance.

The interface should never increase stress.

Prana exists to ensure every interaction feels natural, supportive, and effortless.

It gives life to the product without demanding attention.

---

## Our Philosophy

Prana believes that great healthcare design should become almost invisible.

The interface should quietly guide people rather than distract them.

Users should focus on their health—not on learning how to use an application.

Good design removes confusion.

Great design builds confidence.

---

## Core Principles

Prana is built upon seven principles.

- Calm over Chaos
- Simplicity over Complexity
- Guidance over Instruction
- Accessibility by Default
- Consistency builds Trust
- Motion with Purpose
- Beauty through Clarity

These principles influence every design decision within Jeevan.

---

## What Prana Defines

Prana establishes the standards for:

- Color System
- Typography
- Spacing
- Grid System
- Iconography
- Illustration Language
- Motion System
- Components
- Interaction Patterns
- Accessibility
- Design Tokens

Every visual decision should be consistent with these standards.

## Design Goal

Our goal is not to create beautiful interfaces.

Our goal is to create healthcare experiences that people trust.

Beauty is a consequence of thoughtful design—not the objective.

---

## Our Promise

Every decision made within Prana should answer one question.

> Does this help someone feel safer, calmer, or more confident?

If the answer is no, the decision should be reconsidered.

---

## Closing Statement

Prana is not a style guide.

It is the visual language through which Jeevan communicates with people.

Technology changes.

Design trends change.

Our commitment to thoughtful, human-centered healthcare experiences does not.
