# Product Roadmap

**Version:** 0.1
**Last Updated:** 30 June 2026
**Status:** Active

---

# Purpose

This document defines the long-term roadmap for Jeevan.

The roadmap provides direction for the evolution of the platform while ensuring every phase remains focused on solving real healthcare problems.

The roadmap is intended to guide product planning and prioritization.

It is not a commitment to release dates.

---

# Product Philosophy

Jeevan will be built incrementally.

Each phase must deliver meaningful value on its own before expanding into additional healthcare domains.

Quality is prioritized over speed.

Reliability is prioritized over feature count.

---

# Roadmap Principles

Every phase must:

- Solve a real problem.
- Be independently valuable.
- Improve accessibility.
- Increase trust.
- Maintain simplicity.

A new phase should never reduce the quality of an existing experience.

---

# What We Will Not Do

We will not expand simply to increase feature count.

Every addition to Jeevan must strengthen the platform rather than complicate it.

If a feature does not support the mission, it will not be built.

---

# Closing Statement

The roadmap represents the direction of Jeevan.

It is expected to evolve over time as we learn from users and better understand their needs.

The destination remains constant.

The path may change.
