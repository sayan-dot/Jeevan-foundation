# Purpose

This document provides a high-level overview of Jeevan as a product.

It explains what Jeevan is, who it is built for, what problems it solves, and the principles behind its design.

This document serves as the starting point for anyone joining the project.

---

# What is Jeevan?

Jeevan is a healthcare platform designed to simplify healthcare for everyone through intuitive, accessible, and human-centered technology.

The first version of Jeevan focuses on medication management for elderly users.

However, medication reminders represent only the beginning of a much larger healthcare ecosystem.

The long-term goal is to create a trusted healthcare companion that assists users throughout their healthcare journey.

---

# Product Vision

Jeevan aims to become India's most trusted healthcare companion by making healthcare easier to understand, easier to manage, and easier to access.

The product should feel calm, reliable, and supportive rather than technical or overwhelming.

---

# The Problem

Healthcare today is often fragmented.

People struggle to:

- Remember medicines
- Manage prescriptions
- Track appointments
- Support elderly family members
- Understand healthcare information
- Navigate complex healthcare systems

Existing healthcare applications often prioritize features over usability, making them difficult for many users—especially elderly people.

Jeevan exists to solve these problems through thoughtful design and accessible technology.

---

# Our Solution

Jeevan simplifies healthcare by focusing on clarity instead of complexity.

Instead of overwhelming users with information, the product guides them toward the next meaningful action.

The experience should always feel:

- Simple
- Calm
- Friendly
- Trustworthy
- Accessible

Primary Users

- Individuals with limited digital literacy

- Hospitals
- Clinics

# Product Principles

Jeevan is built around five product goals.

## Simplicity

Healthcare should be easy to understand.

---

## Accessibility

Every user should feel comfortable using the product regardless of age or language.

---

## Guidance

The product should guide users instead of expecting them to learn complicated interfaces.

---

## Trust

Every interaction should increase confidence.

---

## Reliability

Healthcare products must be dependable.

Users should feel confident relying on Jeevan every day.

---

# Current Product Scope

Version 1 focuses on medication management.

Included features:

- Medicine reminders
- Reminder notifications
- Medicine scheduling
- Reminder history
- User profile
- Settings

Everything else is considered future scope.

---

# Future Scope

The Jeevan ecosystem may eventually include:

- Appointment management
- Emergency assistance
- Family caregiver support
- Medical records
- AI health companion (sarathi)
- Regional language support
- Voice guidance
- Doctor integration
- Pharmacy integration
- Wearable device support

Future expansion should always remain aligned with the company's mission and vision.

---

# Product Identity

Jeevan should never feel like hospital software.

It should feel like a trusted companion that quietly helps people manage their health with confidence.

Technology should remain invisible.

Care should remain visible.

---

# Success

The product succeeds when people feel safer, healthier, and more confident managing their healthcare.

The goal is not to maximize screen time.

The goal is to improve quality of life.
