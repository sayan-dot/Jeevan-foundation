# Target Users

**Version:** 0.1
**Last Updated:** 30 June 2026
**Status:** Active

---

# Purpose

This document defines the target audience for Jeevan.

Every design decision, feature, interaction, and user experience should begin by identifying which user group it serves.

If a feature does not benefit one of these users, it should be reconsidered.

---

# Primary Users

## Elderly Individuals

Age Group

55+

Needs

- Remember medicines
- Reduce healthcare confusion
- Large and readable interface
- Easy navigation
- Minimal learning curve
- Confidence using technology

Challenges

- Limited digital literacy
- Difficulty reading small text
- Difficulty understanding English
- Difficulty remembering schedules
- Fear of making mistakes

Primary Goal

Stay healthy independently.

# Accessibility Considerations

Products should assume that users may have:

- Limited English proficiency
- Poor eyesight
- Hearing difficulties
- Memory challenges
- Limited smartphone experience
- Anxiety around technology

The interface should reduce these barriers wherever possible.

---

# User Expectations

Users expect Jeevan to be:

- Easy to understand
- Reliable
- Fast
- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Helpful

Not complicated.

Not technical.

Not overwhelming.

---

# Who We Are NOT Building For

Jeevan is not designed for:

- Power users seeking advanced health analytics
- Fitness enthusiasts looking for workout tracking
- Medical professionals requiring clinical software
- Hospital administration
- Complex Electronic Health Record systems

These needs may be addressed by future products but are outside the scope of Jeevan's primary mission.

---

# Design Implications

Knowing our users means:

- Larger touch targets
- More visuals, less text
- Simple navigation
- Consistent layouts
- Clear feedback
- Voice assistance (future)
- Regional language support
- Minimal cognitive load

Every screen should be designed with these users in mind.

---

# Closing Statement

The success of Jeevan depends on understanding the people it serves.

We are not designing for ourselves.

We are designing for the people who trust us with their health.
