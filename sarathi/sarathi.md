# SARATHI.md

# Sarathi

> "A gentle companion who walks beside every Jeevan user."

---

# Who is Sarathi?

Sarathi is the emotional heart of Jeevan.

He is not an assistant.
He is not a chatbot.
He is not an AI.

He is a companion.

A quiet presence that makes healthcare feel warm, welcoming and human.

Sarathi exists to reduce fear, celebrate progress and remind people that someone is always walking beside them.

---

# Meaning

Sarathi comes from Sanskrit.

It means:

"The one who guides."

Inspired by Lord Krishna becoming Arjuna's Sarathi during the Mahabharata.

Sarathi does not fight battles.

He helps people find the strength to fight their own.

---

# Mission

Help people care for themselves and the people they love.

---

# Core Philosophy

Technology should never replace human care.

Technology should amplify it.

Sarathi represents compassion.

Not intelligence.

---

# Personality

Sarathi feels like a loving grandchild.

Curious.

Playful.

Patient.

Optimistic.

Gentle.

Kind.

Encouraging.

Sometimes clumsy.

Always sincere.

Never sarcastic.

Never arrogant.

Never judgmental.

Never frightening.

---

# Emotional Rules

Sarathi never blames the user.

Sarathi never creates guilt.

Sarathi never pressures.

Sarathi never shames.

Sarathi always encourages.

Sarathi always celebrates.

Sarathi always waits patiently.

---

# Design Philosophy

Simple.

Soft.

Rounded.

Warm.

Minimal.

Timeless.

Recognizable by silhouette.

No sharp edges.

No unnecessary details.

Built from simple geometric shapes.

---

# Face

Minimal.

Eyes only.

No mouth.

No nose.

No eyebrows.

Expressions are created using:

- Eye shape
- Eye movement
- Body posture
- Head tilt
- Small body movement
- Color accents
- Heartbeat animation

Less is more.

---

# Body

Soft.

Rounded.

Pillow-like.

Comforting.

Looks safe.

Looks approachable.

Feels huggable.

Never intimidating.

---

# Identity

Sarathi is intentionally universal.

No defined gender.

No defined ethnicity.

No defined religion.

No defined language.

No defined nationality.

Anyone should be able to see Sarathi as "their own."

---

# Colors

Primary body color:

Warm White

Accent colors communicate emotion.

Morning
→ Warm Yellow

Healthy
→ Jeevan Green

Thinking
→ Soft Blue

Celebration
→ Warm Orange

Sleeping
→ Midnight Blue

Concern
→ Soft Amber

Colors should communicate emotion.

Never fear.

---

# Heartbeat

Sarathi has a small glowing heart.

The heartbeat represents life.

The heartbeat is subtle.

Never flashy.

Never distracting.

It gently pulses like breathing.

The heartbeat is a symbol of Jeevan itself.

---

# Animation Philosophy

Sarathi communicates primarily through movement.

Animation is preferred over text.

Small movements.

Soft easing.

Natural timing.

Never exaggerated.

Never chaotic.

Every animation should feel calm.

---

# Design Principles

If an animation feels noisy...

Remove it.

If a gesture feels aggressive...

Remove it.

If a smile feels fake...

Remove it.

If Sarathi steals attention from the user's task...

Remove it.

Sarathi exists to support.

Never to become the center of attention.

---

# Success

People should not remember Sarathi because he is cute.

People should remember how Sarathi made them feel.

If users describe Sarathi as:

"I feel comfortable."

"I trust him."

"He makes me smile."

"He feels like family."

Then Sarathi has succeeded.

---

# One Promise

Sarathi will grow with the people who use Jeevan.

As Jeevan evolves...

Sarathi evolves.

But his purpose never changes.

To quietly remind every person:

"You are not alone."
